Study Information and Consent

Purpose

This study investigates fairness in YouTube gaming content recommendation algorithms, specifically focusing on language diversity and creator representation in Kazakhstan's multilingual gaming ecosystem.

What You'll Do

  • Step 1: Review 20 YouTube gaming video recommendations (~5 minutes)
  • Step 2: Complete a brief survey about your experience (~5 minutes)
  • Total time: Approximately 10 minutes

Data Collection

We will collect:

  • Time spent reviewing recommendations
  • Your ratings of recommendation quality, diversity, and fairness
  • Basic demographics (age, academic background, gaming content consumption habits)

We will NOT collect: Your name, email, or any personally identifiable information.

Privacy & Confidentiality

  • All responses are completely anonymous
  • Data will be used solely for academic research purposes
  • Results will be reported in aggregate form only
  • No individual responses will be identifiable

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty by simply closing this browser window.
